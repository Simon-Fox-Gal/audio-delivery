Test protocol

These files are not presented as a music release. They are a compact experimental audio set for comparing the perceptual and field-related effects of a fixed 64 Hz carrier under increasing 8 Hz isochronal amplitude modulation.

Carrier frequency

64 Hz continuous tonal component.

Modulation frequency

8 Hz isochronal amplitude modulation.

Variable parameter

Modulation depth: 0%, 10%, 20%, 30%.
